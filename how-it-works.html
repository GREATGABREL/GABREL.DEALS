The Deal Process

01. Vetting & Inquiry

Whether you are a founder or creator, the process starts with a vetting session to understand current needs, past performance, and long term objectives.

I identify partners where the creator's audience or skill set matches the founder's specific protocol stage and target demographic.

03. Terms & Incentive Alignment

I facilitate the negotiation to ensure both parties are properly incentivized. Deals are structured around execution and results, not just promise.

04. Onboarding & Launch

A smooth handoff where communication channels are established and the first phase of execution begins under clear guidelines.
